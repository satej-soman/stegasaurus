What is stegAsaurus? (we spelled it that way on purpose!)

stegAsaurus is a service to quickly and easily share steganographically encoded messages over Dropbox.

Steganography is the practice of hiding text in images. The bytes comprising the payload image are modulated to hide the message in question.

In encode mode, select an image from your Dropbox account, provide a message to conceal, and drop the resultant back image in your Dropbox account.

In decode mode, simply drop a Dropbox URL for a steganographic image and recover the message.

stegAsaurus is powered by stepic and flask.
